How I work

Three rules I don't break.

01

Find the friction first.

Before building anything, I find where the process actually breaks. The fix is usually buried three steps upstream of where it hurts.

02

Ship, don't prototype.

A working thing in production beats a beautiful deck every time. I'd rather hand you something that runs on Tuesday than a plan for someday.

03

Translate both ways.

I sit between the people who have the problem and the systems that solve it — and I speak both languages without losing the plot in either.
